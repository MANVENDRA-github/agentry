---
description: Write or update documentation, via the doc-writer agent.
argument-hint: [file, module, or what to document]
---

Invoke the `doc-writer` agent to document: $ARGUMENTS

If $ARGUMENTS is empty, ask what should be documented — a file, a module, a public API, or a guide — before delegating. $ARGUMENTS may be a file path, a module or directory, a symbol name, or a free-form description of what needs documenting.
